How zeAC works

Simply refill tank with water every morning.

The evaporation of water from the micro porous surfaces of the cylinder material cools the room and the low vapor pressure of the cylinder dehumidifies the room.

More cooling happens with the absorption of heat into the cold cylinder body. Plants, nourished by the constant draw of vapour and nutrients, digest CO2 and other gases, while supplying fresh oxygen. You end up with a micro-climate of stable humidity and cool comfort, all day.

Micro porous ceramic cylinder is used for the evaporation cooling effect.

Using basic thermodynamic principles zeAC is able to cool or heat a room via latent vaporization or condensation heat. The unit functions in two different ways depending on seasons and ambient temperature.

zeAC draws vapour from the room to the cylinder at any temperature.

Powerful, natural air purification is done with living green plants.

These plants are recommended because, compared other plants, they absorb more CO2 and other gases that are not good for you, and supply more oxygen to refresh the air in your room.

zeACs cool as much space as possible within its radius.

zeAC does not work like conventional AC. zeACs cool as much space as possible within its radius. Having one in a house with multiple rooms will result in less cooling overall in the room where it is located and the other rooms will have a slightly lower temperature and humidity.
