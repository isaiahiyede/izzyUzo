About Us

Shipping made easy and hassle free

We are a logistics chain service providers. We provide air freight with a personal touch, handling air charters and sea cargo with professionalism and ease. We also provide co-ordinated shipments to/from sea port areas. Documentation, customs clearance, warehousing, trucking and inland freight services, as well as shipside deliveries. When speed is what you need our express delivery service will make this possible. All these we offer at competive prices and rates
